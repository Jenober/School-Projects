Project 2

After last weeks battle with the Kolbold. The player is taken hostage and forced to fight in an arena. The player fights until he is dead or until the monsters run out. Each battle is pretty much the same. The combatants take turns hurting each other until one has no more HP left. Then there is a day of rest, followed by the next battle.
